Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Simple Summary

This site collects zero personal information about you. Nothing is sent to any server. Everything stays on your own device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location or IP address
  • No browsing history or behaviour tracking
  • No cookies (except those your browser creates locally)
  • No analytics data
  • No payment information

What Is Stored on Your Device

Your browser's localStorage (storage space on your own device) saves:

  • Your city selection (e.g., Windsor, London)
  • Your device preferences (e.g., iPad, iPhone)
  • Your progress through modules (which checkboxes you have ticked)
  • Your accessibility settings (font size, light/dark mode)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser history or localStorage.

Third-Party Links

This Site links to YouTube and other third-party services. Those services have their own privacy policies. We do not control them and are not responsible for their data practices.

Children's Privacy

This Site is designed for adults (seniors). We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
