Canada Revenue Agency (Real Contact)

If you receive a suspicious call from someone claiming to be from the CRA and you want to verify, call the CRA directly at the number below. Never use a phone number given to you by a suspicious caller.

1-800-959-8281 (Individual tax enquiries)

Website: canada.ca/en/revenue-agency
