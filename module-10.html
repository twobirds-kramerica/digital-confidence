The Instacart app icon is a green shopping cart with an orange carrot. Find it in the Apple App Store (for iPhone/iPad) or Google Play Store (for Android). It is free to download.

Getting Started with Instacart (Step by Step)

Step 1: Download and Create an Account

1 Open the App Store (iPhone/iPad) or Google Play Store (Android). Search for "Instacart." Download the free app.
2 Tap Sign Up. Enter your email address and create a password. You will receive a verification email â€” tap the link inside it.
3 Add your payment method â€” a credit or debit card. The number is stored securely and encrypted. Instacart uses the same security as major Canadian banks.
4 Allow location access when prompted. This shows you which stores deliver to your neighbourhood. You can always change your address in Settings.

Step 2: Placing Your First Order

1 Choose Your Store: Tap the store you normally shop at (e.g., "Loblaws" or "Metro"). You will see their full product catalogue.
2 Add Items: Use the search bar to find products (e.g., type "2% milk" or "whole wheat bread"). Or browse by category â€” Produce, Dairy, Bakery, etc. Tap + Add to put items in your cart.
3 Review Your Cart: Tap the cart icon. You will see your full order and the total cost â€” delivery fee, service fee, and suggested tip all shown separately, before you pay anything.
4 Choose Delivery Time: "As soon as possible" (usually 1â€“2 hours) or schedule for a specific time when you will be home.
5 Add Special Instructions: You can type notes like "Please knock loudly â€” I am hard of hearing" or "Leave groceries at the door." Your shopper will read these.
6 Place Your Order: Review the final total one more time, then tap "Place Order." You will get text and app updates as your shopper selects your items and when they are on their way.
