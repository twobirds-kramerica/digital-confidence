Welcome to the Digital Confidence Centre

Thank you for being one of the first people to try the Digital Confidence Centre. You are part of a small, trusted group helping us make this programme the best it can be for seniors across Ontario.

What This Is

The Digital Confidence Centre is a free, self-paced digital learning programme designed specifically for people who want to feel safer and more confident using their iPad, iPhone, or other devices. Built with real seniors in Ontario, for real seniors in Ontario — in plain language, at your pace, with no jargon and no pressure.

There are eleven main lessons, a bonus camera lesson, and a scam-spotting practice simulator. You can start anywhere, take breaks, and come back whenever you like. Your progress is saved automatically on your device.

How to Get Started

  1. Visit the site: twobirds-kramerica.github.io/digital-confidence
  2. Choose your city when prompted — this personalises your local support resources.
  3. Tell us which devices you use — your iPad, iPhone, computer, or phone — so we show you the right instructions.
  4. Start with Module 1 — take your time, there is no rush, and you can always go back.

What We Would Love to Know

As you use the site, please share your honest thoughts using the "Ideas & Feedback" button on any page. We want to know:

  • What was confusing or unclear?
  • What helped you most?
  • What would you like to see added?

Your feedback directly shapes the final version of this programme. Every comment is read and considered. There is no such thing as a silly suggestion — if something puzzled you, it probably puzzles others too.

Need Help?

If you ever feel stuck or uncertain, these two free services are there for you:

Connected Canadians: 1-855-808-0505

Free, one-on-one help by phone. Funded by the Government of Canada. Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm ET.

Cyber-Seniors: 1-844-217-3057

Free tech help from trained senior volunteers — in plain language, at your pace.

A Note From Us

There are no silly questions here. You cannot break anything. Every step forward — no matter how small — is a win worth celebrating. We are genuinely grateful you are here, and we hope this programme gives you the confidence to use your devices with ease and enjoyment.
